Veteran Founded and Owned

Countdown City Yotas (CCY) is San Antonio's premier community for Toyota enthusiasts. Born in the heart of the 210, we are a family-oriented group dedicated to celebrating the "Texas Built" heritage of our trucks. From local trail rides and off-road adventures to community service and high-profile meets like CarFest or Battle of The Alamo, CCY is about more than just vehicles; it's about the people behind the wheel and the city we call home.
